Basketball

For example, I am interested in playing basketball. First of all, I will learn to play basketball and learn the rules of basketball by watching basketball games (i.e. NBA, CBA). Before playing basketball, I will do enough "hardware preparation", such as buying good quality basketball and basketball shoes. Then, I will search the Internet for some basketball skills as "software preparation", such as dribbling skills, passing skills, shooting skills and so on. In addition, I found a coach to train me to play basketball and kept it up for a year. I spend a lot of time practicing the basics of basketball on my own, and during this time I play with equal players one-on-one or many-to-many. In actual combat, I found that I could improve my basketball skills rapidly and even make many friends. Gradually, I found that playing basketball can not only strengthen physical fitness, but also improve their adaptability, communication ability and team cooperation ability. While improving my basketball level, I continue to enjoy the fun of learning basketball skills, and also enjoy the fun brought by playing with people of higher level. Therefore, I think I love basketball, love the process of learning to play basketball, and enjoy the fun that playing basketball brings to my life.
